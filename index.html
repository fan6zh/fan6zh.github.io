Dr Fan Zhang

Personal Robotics Lab
Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering
Imperial College London
South Kensington Campus, London SW7 2AZ

I am a Research Associate in the Personal Robotics Lab, Imperial College London. I received my Ph.D. degree also from Imperial College London (advised by Prof. Yiannis Demiris, Royal Academy of Engineering Chair in Emerging Technologies).

I am currently working on robot-assisted dressing for impaired patients. I am also working for the D-RISK and Multidisciplinary University Research Initiatives (MURI) Program. My research interests cover many aspects of learning algorithms applied to assistive robotics, including perception and manipulation, sim2real transfer learning, reinforcement learning, among other.

I have been awarded The Queen Mary UK Best PhD in Robotics Award 2020 1st place! My research has been reported by Bloomberg, The Times. Please refer to my CV for more information. More information about robot-assisted dressing can be found here.

While not working, I enjoy hiking, travelling, playing squash and ukulele.
