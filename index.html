Fan Zhang

Honda Research Institute EU
Frankfurt, Germany

I am a senior research scientist and a project leader at Honda Research Institute EU. I am also a visiting researcher at Imperial College London. I was an Eric and Wendy Schmidt AI in Science Postdoctoral Fellow funded by Schmidt Futures. I received my Ph.D. degree from Imperial College London (advised by Prof. Yiannis Demiris, Personal Robotics Lab).

My research interests cover many aspects of machine learning algorithms applied to general robot manipulation. I have been awarded The Queen Mary UK Best PhD in Robotics Award 2020 1st place! My research has been accepted at Science Robotics and reported by Bloomberg, The Times.
